MuleBuy

A fashion affiliate marketing tool that curates product images and outfit inspiration, then publishes approved content to your connected TikTok account.

What it does

MuleBuy helps the operator prepare fashion affiliate posts — images, product details, captions and affiliate links — and publish them to their own TikTok profile. Content is only posted after the operator approves it; nothing is published automatically without review.

How it works

Step 1
Connect account

The operator authorizes their own TikTok account so the tool can publish on their behalf.

Step 2
Build draft

The tool prepares curated images, product details, captions and affiliate links for review.

Step 3
Human approval

The operator previews each draft and chooses to approve, reject, or regenerate it.

Step 4
Publish

Only approved content is uploaded to the connected TikTok profile via the Content Posting API.
